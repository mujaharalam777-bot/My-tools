❓ FAQ & How To Use

How to use this calculator?

Simply enter your name in the first box, and your partner's/crush's name in the second. Select your genders and hit Calculate. If you use DOB mode, enter birthdays for astrological matching!

Is the Love Calculator accurate?

This tool uses a deterministic character-based algorithm and numerology. It's meant for fun and entertainment! Real love is based on trust, not algorithms. 😉

What is Secret Crush mode?

It hides the second name while typing so your friends looking over your shoulder can't see who you are calculating with!
