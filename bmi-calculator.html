Frequently Asked Questions

What is BMI?

BMI = Body Mass Index. It tells if your weight is healthy according to your height.

What is a normal BMI range?

Normal BMI range is 18.5 to 24.9

Is BMI accurate for athletes?

BMI doesn't consider muscle mass. Athletes may have high BMI but be healthy.
