Frequently Asked Questions

How to remove duplicate lines online instantly?

To use our remove duplicate lines online tool, simply paste your text, keyword list, or CSV into the input box. Configure your settings (like Case Sensitivity or Trim Spaces), choose your preferred "Keep Mode", and click the "Remove Duplicates" button. Your clean, deduplicated list will appear on the right instantly, ready to copy or download.

Can this tool act as a CSV duplicate remover?

Yes! This tool functions as a highly capable csv duplicate remover and remove duplicate excel utility. When you paste comma-separated values, the tool automatically detects the format and reveals a dropdown menu. You can then select a specific column to evaluate for duplicates, leaving the rest of your CSV row data intact based on the selected Keep Mode.

What is the "Smart Count Group" and "Fuzzy Match" feature?

The Smart Count feature transforms this into a robust keyword list cleaner and remove duplicate words application. Instead of just deleting, it outputs the unique line alongside the number of times it appeared (e.g., "seo tools - 5x"). The Fuzzy Match toggle uses a Levenshtein distance algorithm (85% similarity) to detect and group slight typos (like "seo tool" and "SEO Tools") as the same duplicate entity.
