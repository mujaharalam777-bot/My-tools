How a Random Password Generator Works

Every time you click the "Generate" button, our script uses the cryptographically secure window.crypto.getRandomValues() web standard. This is different from normal randomizers, ensuring that your generated keys have pure, unpredictable physical entropy.

A random selection of uppercase letters, numbers, and symbols is instantly compiled into a customizable string size.

The Benefits of Brute-Force Time Estimation

Our algorithm simulates a supercomputer trying trillions of combinations per second. By displaying the estimated time required to hack your key, you can visually see the performance of your new secure password before applying it to your accounts.
