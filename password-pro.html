Password Strength & Brute Force FAQ

How is my password strength analyzed?

Our algorithm evaluates the dynamic variety of character spaces used in your password (lowercase, uppercase, numerals, and special characters) to compute its exact mathematical entropy in bits, then simulates brute force attempts.
